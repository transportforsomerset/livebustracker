Service 1E - The Buses of Somerset (FBOS)

This service is a one-way loop, which extends part of the service 1 route, but does not include the former service 2 route.

Starting and ending at Taunton's Silk Mills Park and Ride, then follows the Park and Ride route to Musgrove Park Hospital, towards Taunton's Parade (stand H) in the Town Centre, and travels towards Taunton Railway Station, up through Cheddon Road, around Wellsprings and Pyrland, and back into Town via the Railway Station, serving the Parade again (stand J), then back along the Park and Ride route, serving the Hospital and Silk Mills.

The final bus of the evening ends at Taunton's Parade, and does not serve the hospital or Silk Mills.
