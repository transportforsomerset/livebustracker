First Bus Somerset (FBOS)

First Bus Somerset, formally branded as "The Buses of Somerset", operate services in and around Somerset.

They have their main depot based in Taunton, with an additional outstation based in Minehead. They previously had depots in Bridgwater and Yeovil

They mostly service the County Town of Taunton, with services also in Bridgwater and Yeovil.
