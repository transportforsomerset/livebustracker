Service 24 - Stagecoach South West (SDVN)

This service is a small one-way heavy service, with three early morning services coming out of Tiverton towards Taunton, and three late evening returning services from Taunton to Tiverton. This service does not operate during the day.

Service 24 is operated by Stagecoach South West

The buses used to operate service 24 are also used to operate the Taunton Park and Ride, with the end of service 24 becoming the start of the Park and Ride service, and the end of the Park and Ride service becoming the start of service 24.
