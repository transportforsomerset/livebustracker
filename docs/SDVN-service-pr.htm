Taunton Park & Ride (PR) - Stagecoach South West (SDVN)

The Taunton Park & Ride service (PR) operates between the Silk Mills and Gateway car parks, stopping at Musgrove Park Hospital and Taunton town centre. The single fare is capped at Â£1. Parking is free at the two Park and Ride sites.

Services operate every 20 minutes between 6am and 7pm during the week, and between 8am and 6pm on Saturday, using double decker buses. There is no Sunday service.

The Taunton Park and Ride Service is operated by Stagecoach South West.
