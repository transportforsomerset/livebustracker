Service 7 - The Buses of Somerset (FBOS)

This service is a one-way loop, starting and ending at Taunton's Parade (Stand J, beside the Wildwood Restaurant) in the Town Centre, and travels towards Parkfield Road, Collage Way, Queensway, Comeytrowe Lane, Deane Drive, Galmington Road, then back into Town (Stand H, by Fat Face), towards Taunton Railway Station, then finally back into Taunton Town Centre (Stand J).

The service operates roughly every 50 minutes during the week, and every 45 minutes on a Saturday. There is no Sunday service.
