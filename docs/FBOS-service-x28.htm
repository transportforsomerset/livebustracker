Service X28 - The Buses of Somerset (FBOS)

This service is a two-way route, between Taunton Castle Way and Minehead Butlins, with three trips a day (Mon-Fri) in each direction. The X28 does not stop at Bishops Lydeard or Watchet.

Service X28 is operated by The Buses of Somerset.

The single fare is capped at £1.50 for stops within Taunton, with the £3 fare cap in place for the remainder of the route.
