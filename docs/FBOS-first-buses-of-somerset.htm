First (The) Buses of Somerset (FBOS)

First Buses of Somerset, often referred to as The Buses of Somerset, operate services in and around Somerset.

They have two main depots, based in Taunton and Yeovil, with an additional outstation based in Minehead.

They mostly service the County Town of Taunton, with services also in Bridgwater and Yeovil.
